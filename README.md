This is my fan made TES project.

Doing this has tought me quite a bit about how it works.
Ive done sone work with arduinos to make sensors,
but that is the extent of my experience.
The entierity of this game's engine and a lot of the places
are generated by Claude AI
I did lots of pixel art, wrote the story, and wrote the music.
With that out of the way, let me present:

Stormcrown: a fnamade TES project

The primary inspirition for this game was what if 
there was an Elder Scrolls game that played like
classic Fallout.
I started with this idea, and I decided that I wanted to 
tell the story of the rise of Tiber Septim.
I chose this period of history to discuss because 
the Brass Tower walks, and the Dragon breaks.
I thought this would give me lots of narrative freedom
and not invalidate player experience.
There is a lot of fun and goofy stuff
for the player to discover.

There is bound to be many bugs with this game.
As I am but a single man, i cannot efficiently play test
this game.
I am looking for feedback from anyone who actually plays it

Enjoy :)
